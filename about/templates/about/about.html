About Us

House Store & More + Techno Tech Co. Ltd - Smart Housing Solutions and Equipment Services Worldwide

House Store & More + Techno Tech Co. Ltd specializes in providing innovative equipment solutions for creating pop-up-style smart houses and rooms. With the ability to transform small to medium-sized spaces in just minutes, we offer a unique and efficient approach to modern living across the globe.

Established in 2014 as a family-owned and operated business, we have steadily grown our presence worldwide. In 2023, we are excited to announce the launch of our Home Equipment Webstore, further expanding our offerings and making it easier for customers to access our cutting-edge products and services.

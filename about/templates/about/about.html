About Us

Techno Tech Multi-Sensory Solutions is a Sensory equipment provision service operating in the North East of Ireland.

Techno Tech, can create a pop-up style sensory room in virtually any small to medium-sized space in a matter of minutes.

Our Irish-owned, family-run, business has been in operation since 2018. As the company continues to grow, 2023 will see further expansion with the introduction of our sensory equipment webstore.

Webstore

Our new e-commerce webstore will allow our existing customer base, as well as new service users, to purchase supplementary products which can be used to enhance the user's experience in any existing sensory space, or alongside our Mobile Sensory Environments service.
